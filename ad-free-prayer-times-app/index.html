Why ad-free matters for salah

A prayer app is opened at sensitive moments of the day: before Fajr, during work, before leaving home, or while checking the next prayer. Waqto Salat keeps that experience simple. The app is designed around prayer essentials instead of advertising surfaces, popups, or locked features.

What is included

Waqto Salat includes prayer times, dynamic salah reminders, Qibla direction, Android home screen widgets, per-prayer calibration, and multilingual support. The goal is not to add every possible Islamic app feature. The goal is to make the daily prayer flow easier to follow with fewer distractions.

Best fit

Waqto Salat is a good fit if you want a focused Muslim prayer times app for Android, prefer a quiet interface, need reminders, and want quick access to the next prayer from your phone or widget.

What makes it different

Many prayer apps become busy because they try to combine too many unrelated features, ads, and promotional surfaces. Waqto Salat keeps the promise narrower: prayer times, reminders that move with salah times, Qibla direction, widgets, and calibration. That narrower focus is the point. It makes the app easier to trust during the parts of the day when you only want to check the next prayer and move on.

What a useful salah reminder should do

A salah reminder app should make the next prayer easier to notice, not harder to manage. Waqto Salat keeps reminders tied to the prayer schedule, so a reminder before Fajr or after Dhuhr can move with the actual salah time instead of staying fixed like a normal alarm.

Built around the full prayer flow

Reminders work best when they live beside accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, widgets, and calibration controls. Waqto Salat combines these pieces in one Android app, making it easier to check the time, prepare for salah, and align settings with your location and community.

Simple, free, and ad-free

Waqto Salat is free to download and is designed without ads or locked prayer essentials. That matters for reminders because the app should feel trustworthy when it appears on your phone throughout the day.

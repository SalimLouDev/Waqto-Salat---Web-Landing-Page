Waqto Salat - Dynamic Salah Reminders for Android

Waqto Salat is an ad-free Android prayer times app with dynamic salah reminders, Qibla direction, home screen widgets, prayer time calibration, and multilingual support.

Set reminders before or after Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, or Isha once. Waqto Salat updates them automatically as daily prayer times change.

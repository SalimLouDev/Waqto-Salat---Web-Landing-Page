Prayer times widget for Android

Waqto Salat helps keep the next prayer visible with Android home screen widgets, so you can check salah times without opening the full app every time.

Glanceable prayer information

A prayer times widget is useful because it lowers friction. Instead of opening an app, searching through a list, or waiting for a notification, you can glance at your Android home screen and see the prayer information you need.

Designed for repeated checks

Prayer times are checked repeatedly throughout the day. Waqto Salat keeps widgets connected to a broader calm experience with prayer times, salah reminders, Qibla direction, calibration, multilingual support, and an ad-free interface.

Who should use it

The widget feature is useful if you want the next prayer to stay visible while working, studying, commuting, or planning your day around salah.

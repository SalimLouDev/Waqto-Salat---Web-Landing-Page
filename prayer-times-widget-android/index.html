Glanceable prayer information

A prayer times widget is useful because it lowers friction. Instead of opening an app, searching through a list, or waiting for a notification, you can glance at your Android home screen and see the prayer information you need.

Designed for repeated checks

Prayer times are checked repeatedly throughout the day. Waqto Salat keeps widgets connected to a broader calm experience with prayer times, salah reminders, Qibla direction, calibration, multilingual support, and an ad-free interface.

Who should use it

The widget feature is useful if you want the next prayer to stay visible while working, studying, commuting, or planning your day around salah.

Why widgets help consistency

Prayer times are not checked once a day. They shape the rhythm of the whole day. A widget keeps that rhythm visible without opening another screen, searching for the next prayer, or waiting for a notification. In Waqto Salat, widgets work together with dynamic salah reminders, Qibla direction, and calibration so the home screen supports the same calm prayer flow as the app itself.

Common questions

Does Waqto Salat include Android widgets?

Yes. Waqto Salat includes home screen widgets for checking prayer times at a glance.

Do widgets replace reminders?

No. Widgets are best for quick visibility, while dynamic reminders help notify you before or after a selected prayer time.
